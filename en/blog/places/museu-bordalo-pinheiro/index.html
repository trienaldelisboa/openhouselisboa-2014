Description

House of Artur Cruz Magalhães in the style of a “Portuguese house”, which received a honourable mention in the 1914 Valmor Prize. The owner was a collector of the work of Rafael Bordalo Pinheiro and three rooms were set aside on the first floor as a museum, with a separate public entrance. This museum opened in 1916. In 1924, the house and collection were donated to Lisbon City Council. The house underwent refurbishment in 2005 and a new museological program.
