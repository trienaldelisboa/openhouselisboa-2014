Description

One of Lisbon’s largest developments, Entrecampos Square is located between the campus of the University of Lisbon and the city’s central business district. The project comprises 650 apartment units, 600 student residences, a large office block and an office tower for the city’s urban innovation agency, 5000 underground parking spaces, a public square and a performing arts centre.
