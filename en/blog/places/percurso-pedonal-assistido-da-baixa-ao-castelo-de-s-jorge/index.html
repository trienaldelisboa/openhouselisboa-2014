Description

The requalification of urban space is a fundamental contribution for the revitalization of this area of the city. The existing pavement was replaced by stone slabs of Lioz limestone, with a honed finishing, agreeable to users, this being the connecting thread in the whole intervention. The Public Lifts Building operates as a natural extension of the urban exterior to the interior, showing naturally its public characteristics that invite people to walk and to enjoy.
