Description

The construction of Centro Cultural de Belém was decided in 1988, to accommodate the Portuguese European Union Presidency, in 1992. Presently the CCB occupies a construction area of 97,000m2, distributed along 60,000m2 and separated by two internal streets, connected by a pedestrian way that connects to the exterior and to Praça do Império. CCB is a small city with gardens, lakes, bridges and small hidden places, where the nobility of the CCB Square stands out.
