Description

The operations to modernize the Alcântara Wastewater Treatment Plant involved the creation of a roof, for environmental protection reasons. The project proposes a roof, that can be inhabited and that contains all the monitoring program and required services. This green roof extends the green slopes of the Alcântara valley, helping to reduce the impact caused by road infrastructure in the area and hiding a huge density of technical areas.
